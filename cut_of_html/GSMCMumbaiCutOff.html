the NEET test results of the state's students

The cutoff for GSMC Mumbai is often higher for general category applicants than for those from reserved categories. This is due to the smaller number of seats allocated to students in the reserved category.

 

How to Determine the GSMC Cut Off

 

The following formula is used to determine the GSMC Mumbai cut off:

 

Cutoff is calculated as (Total Marks of Last Admitted Candidate) / (Number of Seats Available).

For instance, the cutoff would be 600/250 = 2.4 if the last applicant accepted to GSMC Mumbai in 2022 received 600 marks and there were 250 seats remaining. In order to get accepted to GSMC Mumbai in 2023, an applicant would need to have a score that is at least 2.4 times the national average for the NEET exam.
