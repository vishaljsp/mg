Cutoff for AIIMS New Delhi 2023

 

One of India's most esteemed medical institutions is the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS). The National Eligibility Completion Entrance Examination (NEET) is the primary criterion for admission to AIIMS New Delhi. The minimal score an applicant must get in order to be considered for admission to AIIMS New Delhi is known as the cut off.

 

The number of applicants taking the test, the exam's degree of difficulty, and the availability of seats are some of the variables that affect the AIIMS New Delhi cut off. The number of qualifying applicants and the number of seats available will determine how high the cut off will be.

 

The 2023 AIIMS New Delhi cutoff has not yet been made public. However, by examining the cut off for prior years, you may obtain a general notion of the anticipated cut off. The following table lists the categories for the 2022 AIIMS New Delhi cut off:

 

Category Cut Off
General 720
SC/ST/OBC 136
ST – PwD 120
OBC/ SC – PwD 120


It is crucial to remember that the cutoff for the AIIMS New Delhi may alter. Following the conclusion of the counseling process, the ultimate cut off will be made public.

 

AIIMS New Delhi Cutoff Conditions

 

The AIIMS New Delhi cut off is influenced by the following factors:

 

Number of applicants taking the test: The cut off will increase as more applicants take the exam.

test difficulty: If the test is challenging, the cut off will be greater.

Seat availability: The cutoff is also influenced by the amount of MBBS seats that are available at AIIMS New Delhi.
