Top AIIMS in India: A Guide for Medical Aspirants

India is home to some of the best medical colleges in the world, and the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) is at the top of the list. AIIMS colleges are known for their high standards of education, world-class facilities, and distinguished faculty.

History of AIIMS

The first AIIMS was established in New Delhi in 1956. It was founded by the Government of India as an autonomous institution to provide excellence in medical education, research, and patient care. The success of AIIMS Delhi led to the establishment of other AIIMS colleges across India.

How to Apply to AIIMS

Admission to AIIMS colleges is highly competitive. Students must qualify for the National Eligibility Entrance Exam (NEET) in order to be eligible to apply. The NEET is a national-level exam that is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

In addition to qualifying for the NEET, students must also meet the eligibility criteria set by the individual AIIMS colleges. These criteria may vary from college to college, but they typically include requirements such as minimum age, educational qualifications, and language proficiency.
