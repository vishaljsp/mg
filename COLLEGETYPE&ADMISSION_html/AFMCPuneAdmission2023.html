Admission to AFMC Pune 2023

One of India's top medical schools is the Armed Forces Medical College (AFMC). It is a part of the University of Pune and is situated in Pune, Maharashtra. The AFMC provides a variety of allied health courses in addition to undergraduate and graduate medical programs.

 

eligibility requirements

 

The following qualifications must be met in order to get admitted to AFMC Pune:

 

Candidates must have successfully completed the Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English requirements of the 12th grade.

Candidates in the General category must have an overall minimum requirement of 60%. However, the minimal combined percentage for applicants who are SC/ST/OBC is 50%.

Candidates need to have taken the NEET 2023 test.

Application Method

 

Online applications will be accepted for admission to AFMC Pune in 2023. Beginning in July 2023, the application form will be accessible on the AFMC website. The application cost is INR 750 for SC, ST, and OBC applicants and INR 1500 for general category candidates.

 

Choice Process

 

Candidates will be chosen for admission to AFMC Pune depending on how well they performed in the NEET 2023 test and in a subsequent interview. The NEET 2023 test will take place in July 2023. Only the top 1% of applicants who take the NEET 2023 test will likely be accepted because to the very high cut-off for admission to the AFMC Pune.

 

courses provided

 

The following undergraduate and graduate medical programs are available at AFMC Pune:

 

Fees for MBBS, MD, MS, DM, M.Ch, and PhD diploma programmes

 

The following is the fee schedule for 2023 admission to AFMC Pune:

 

INR 4.5 lakhs annually for MBBS

MD/MS: 6.5 lakhs INR annually

DM/M.Ch: 8 lakhs INR annually

PhD: Rs. 3 lakh annually

Diploma programs cost INR 1 lakh annually.

How to Use

 

You should do the following actions to apply for admission to AFMC Pune in 2023:

 

Please go to the AFMC website.

The "Admissions" tab may be selected.

On the "Apply Online" link, click.

Complete the application and submit the required payment.

upload the papers you've scanned.

Send in the application.

Significant Dates

 

The following dates are crucial for 2023 admission to AFMC Pune:

 

Release of the application form: July 2023

NEET 2023 examination date: July 2023 NEET 2023 results announcement date: August 2023 Application form submission deadline: August 2023

AFMC Date of Pune seat allocation: September 2023

Conclusion

 

As a highly esteemed medical school, AFMC Pune has a very tough admissions process. The AFMC Pune should be your first choice if you want to become a doctor. You must, however, be ready to put in a lot of effort and outperform the nation's top pupils.

 

In addition to the aforementioned, the following are some more considerations for applicants to AFMC Pune admission 2023:

 

The application form must be completed completely and truthfully. Any mistakes on the application form might result in the rejection of your request.

The application form must be submitted with all necessary papers scanned and uploaded.

The whole application cost must be paid.

Before the application deadline, the NEET 2023 test must be taken and the results released.
