Admission to GSMC Mumbai 2023

 

One of India's top medical schools is the Gokhale Institute of Medical Sciences (GSMC). It has ties to the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences and is situated in Mumbai. GSMC is one of Mumbai's oldest medical schools; it was established in 1943.

 

The GSMC provides a variety of allied health courses in addition to undergraduate and graduate medical programs. There are other research centers and institutions located at the campus as well.

 

eligibility requirements

 

The following qualifications must be met in order to be admitted to GSMC:

 

Candidates must have successfully completed the Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English requirements of the 12th grade.

Candidates in the General category must have an overall minimum requirement of 60%. However, the minimal combined percentage for applicants who are SC/ST/OBC is 50%.

Candidates need to have taken the NEET 2023 test.

Application Method

 

Online applications will be accepted for admission to GSMC in 2023. Beginning in July 2023, the application form will be accessible on the GSMC website. The application cost is INR 250 for SC/ST/OBC applicants and INR 500 for general category applicants.

 

Choice Process

 

Candidates will be chosen for admission to GSMC depending on how well they performed in the NEET 2023 test. The NEET 2023 test will take place in July 2023. Only the top 1% of applicants who take the NEET 2023 test are likely to be accepted since the cut-off for admission to GSMC is so high.

 

courses provided

 

The following undergraduate and graduate medical programs are available at GSMC:

 

Fees for MBBS, MD, MS, DM, M.Ch, and PhD diploma programmes

 

The following is the cost schedule for GSMC admission in 2023:

 

MBBS: INR 50,000 annually

MD/MS: 7 lakhs INR annually

DM/M.Ch: 8 lakhs INR annually

PhD: Rs. 3 lakh annually

Diploma programs cost INR 1 lakh annually.

How to Use

 

The actions below must be taken in order to apply for admission to GSMC in 2023:

 

Please go to the GSMC website.

The "Admissions" tab may be selected.

On the "Apply Online" link, click.

Complete the application and submit the required payment.

upload the papers you've scanned.

Send in the application.

Significant Dates

 

The following are key dates for GSMC admission in 2023:

 

Release of the application form: July 2023

NEET 2023 examination date: July 2023 NEET 2023 results announcement date: August 2023 Application form submission deadline: August 2023

Date of GSMC seat allocation: September 2023

Conclusion

 

As a highly esteemed medical school, GSMC has a very tough admissions process. GSMC ought to be your #1 choice if you want to become a doctor. You must, however, be ready to put in a lot of effort and outperform the nation's top pupils.

 

In addition to the aforementioned, the following are some other considerations for GSMC admission 2023 applications:

 

The application form must be completed completely and truthfully. Any mistakes on the application form might result in the rejection of your request.

The application form must be submitted with all necessary papers scanned and uploaded.

The whole application cost must be paid.

Before the application deadline, the NEET 2023 test must be taken and the results released.
