Admission to JIPMER Pondicherry 2023

 

One of India's top medical schools is the Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research (JIPMER). It is situated in the southern Indian Union Territory of Puducherry. JIPMER provides a variety of allied health courses in addition to undergraduate and graduate medical programs.

 

eligibility requirements

 

The following are the requirements for admission to JIPMER Pondicherry:

 

Candidates must have successfully completed the Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English requirements of the 12th grade.

The necessary minimum cumulative percentage for candidates in the General category is 50%. However, the minimal combined percentage for applicants who are SC/ST/OBC is 40%.

Candidates need to have taken the NEET 2023 test.

Application Method

 

Admission to JIPMER Pondicherry in 2023 will be via online application alone. Beginning in July 2023, the application form will be accessible on the JIPMER website. The application cost is INR 750 for SC, ST, and OBC applicants and INR 1500 for general category candidates.

 

Choice Process

 

Candidates will be chosen for admission to JIPMER Pondicherry depending on how well they performed in the NEET 2023 test. The NEET 2023 test will take place in July 2023. Only the top 1% of applicants who take the NEET 2023 test will likely be accepted because to the very high cut-off for admission to JIPMER Pondicherry.

 

courses provided

 

The following undergraduate and graduate medical programs are available at JIPMER Pondicherry:

 

Fees for MBBS, MD, MS, DM, M.Ch, and PhD diploma programmes

 

The following is the fee schedule for 2023 admission to JIPMER Pondicherry:

 

INR 4.5 lakhs annually for MBBS

MD/MS: 6.5 lakhs INR annually

DM/M.Ch: 8 lakhs INR annually

PhD: Rs. 3 lakh annually

Diploma programs cost INR 1 lakh annually.

How to Use

 

Follow these procedures to apply for admission to JIPMER Pondicherry in 2023:

 

Check out the JIPMER webpage.

The "Admissions" tab may be selected.

On the "Apply Online" link, click.

Complete the application and submit the required payment.

upload the papers you've scanned.

Send in the application.

Significant Dates

 

The following are key dates for JIPMER Pondicherry admission in 2023:

 

Release of the application form: July 2023

NEET 2023 examination date: July 2023 NEET 2023 results announcement date: August 2023 Application form submission deadline: August 2023

Date of seat allocation for JIPMER Pondicherry: September 2023

Conclusion

 

A highly esteemed medical school with a tough admissions process is JIPMER Pondicherry. JIPMER Pondicherry ought to be your first choice if you want to become a doctor. You must, however, be ready to put in a lot of effort and outperform the nation's top pupils.

 

In addition to the aforementioned, remember the following while submitting an application for admission to JIPMER Pondicherry in 2023:

 

The application form must be completed completely and truthfully. Any mistakes on the application form might result in the rejection of your request.

The application form must be submitted with all necessary papers scanned and uploaded.

The whole application cost must be paid.

Before the application deadline, the NEET 2023 test must be taken and the results released.
